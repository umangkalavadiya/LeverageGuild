BITS Scholar program

Key Information:

The Scholar program at BITS helps high achieving male figure have a easier time at university.

Scholar program scholarship Eligibility Criteria:

  • Student must get admitted to BITS outside management quota.
  • Student has a 75% GPA in previous study.
  • The applicant has to be male.

Scholar program Scholarship Rewards and Benefits:

  • University dicounts 75% of the tuition fees for the whoel duration of study.
  • This scholarship also fetches 30% Discount inside university housing plans.

Scholar program Scholarship Application Process:

  1. Apply to BITS through central link admissions.
  2. There will be a personal statement of 250 words to be submitted upon admission is confirmed.
