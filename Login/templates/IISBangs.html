IIS Banglore Merit Scholarship.

Key Information:

Since the program at IIS Banglore is fully funded this is an one time gift grant.

Merit scholarship Eligibility Criteria:

  • Enrolled students with GPA above 80% should apply.

Merit Scholarship Rewards and Benefits:

  • Provides a one time grant price of 12400 rupees.

Merit Scholarship Application Process:

  1. Selected students with 80% or more GPA should apply through university portal.
