For a world where student come first

IISc Bangalore is a premier research institute in India, offering a Computer Science program for students to pursue higher education and research in the field. The Department of Computer Science and Automation at IISc offers a PhD program, a Master's degree program (M.Tech) and a dual degree program (M.Tech-PhD).

The program covers a wide range of topics in computer science, including algorithms, data structures, computer networks, databases, programming languages, computer graphics, artificial intelligence, and more. Students are trained in cutting-edge research and technologies and are encouraged to work on original research projects under the guidance of faculty members.

The program is highly competitive and attracts top students from across the country. Graduates of the program are well-equipped to work in academia, research, and industry and are highly sought after by top organizations.

IISc Bangalore M.Tech Selection Criteria

Admission to the M.Tech program will be granted on the basis of a valid score in GATE Entrance Exam. For some specializations, candidates are also required to appear in the written test or personal interview rounds as conducted by the institute itself.

Given below is the specialization-wise selection criteria required for IISc Bangalore MTech admissions:

  • Departments /Programmes: Aerospace Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Instrumentation Systems, Materials Engineering

    Selection procedure: Based on 100% GATE score. Tie Breaking: In case of candidates with equal GATE score, their performance in the qualifying examination will be used to resolve the tie.
  • Departments /Programmes: Bioengineering, Climate Science, Earth Science, Electrical Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Electronic Product Design, Electronic Systems Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Microelectronics and VLSI Design, Nano Science and Engineering, Quantum Technology, Robotics and Autonomous Systems, Signal Processing

    Selection procedure: Based on 70% GATE score + 30% performance in interview.
  • Departments /Programmes: Artificial Intelligence, Computer Science and Engineering

    Selection procedure: Based on 70% GATE score + 30% performance in written test.
  • Departments /Programmes: Computational & Data Sciences, Smart Manufacturing

    Selection procedure: Based on 70% GATE score + 30% performance in written test and interview.
