UIUC Champaign scholar program.

Key Information:

The UIUC Champaign scholar program provides phenomenal help to athelets with high grades.

Champaign Scholar program scholarship Eligibility Criteria:

  • Student must be a national level player in any sports UIUC has a team for.
  • Student has a 80% GPA in previous study.

Champaign Scholar program Scholarship Rewards and Benefits:

  • University waives 100% of the tuition fees for the whole duration of study.
  • Best in class sports training and diet at no price.
  • Monthly stipend of 1000 dollars.
  • Living costs is fully covered.
  • Travel fares for any sports events covered completely.

ChampaignScholar program Scholarship Application Process:

  1. Apply and schedule a sports trial in real events.
  2. There will be a personal statement of 250 words to be submitted.
  3. A personal interview regarding sports and study goals.
