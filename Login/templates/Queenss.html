Queen's Mary Deep minds scholarship.

Key Information:

Deep minds scholarship offers excellent fianancial help to under represented groups in artificial intelligence.

Deep Minds scholarship Eligibility Criteria:

  • Student must be a female or of any underrepresented genre of Artificial intelligence.
  • Aspirant with 80% GPA and more should apply.
  • Student must get admitted to an artificial intelligence course at Queens Mary

Deep Minds Scholarship Rewards and Benefits:

  • The University offers 48330 pounds to the qualifying students.
  • Covers their tuition fees and living costs.

Deep Minds Scholarship Application Process:

  1. Apply to Queens Mary and get an offer for Ai based programs.
  2. Write a 250 words personal statement and upload on the website alongside other documents.
