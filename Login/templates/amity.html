For a world where student come first

Amity University established under the aegis of Ritnand Balved Education Foundation, a Non-Profit education trust over 18 years ago. Amity today is one of the most trusted name in Education and has been ranked the No.1 Private University, wherein 95,000 students are pursuing over 240 programs , spread over 1,000 acres of campuses with 4.5 million Sq.ft of built up area. It has a dedicated team of 4000 scientists, Researchers and faculty members who operate in a meritocratic and a professional environment led by 11 former Vice Chancellors, Former Chairman of UGC and Vice Chairman of AICTE.

Amity University offers a Master of Computer Science (MCS) program. It is a two-year full-time program designed to provide advanced knowledge in the field of computer science and technology. The course curriculum covers various aspects of computer science, including computer networks, data structures, algorithms, database systems, software engineering, and more.

Admission to the MCS program at Amity University is based on academic merit and the availability of seats. The university may also consider other criteria, such as work experience and entrance test scores, when making admission decisions.

Eligibility Criteria for Admission

  • Non-Sponsored

    60% aggregate

    60% PCM

  • Sponsored

    55% aggregate

    55% PCM

Note: The aggregate percentage will be calculated based on the marks obtained in English and three academic subjects (excluding second language, Physical Education, Fine Arts, Performing Arts, or any other Vocational/Non Written subjects). It is mandatory for the student to have passed all the subjects of Class XII from a recognized board.
