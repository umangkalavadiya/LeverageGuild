University of Auckland Oceania Scholarship.

Key Information:

The Oceania scholarship provides high achieving students with geneours help to get through their academics.

Oceania scholarship Eligibility Criteria:

  • Be an international student from anywhere but Australia.
  • Have minimum GPA of 80 percent.
  • Have applied to study at U of Auckland by scholarship end date.
  • Satisfy English language entry requirement.

Oceania Scholarship Rewards and Benefits:

  • Discounts 50% of the tuition fees of entire course
  • Provides one time air fare to students.

Oceania Scholarship Application Process:

  1. Selected students should apply through university portal.
  2. Write a 250 words personal statement.
