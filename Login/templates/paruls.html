Parul University Fee Help scholarship

Key Information:

The fee help scholarship helps students back their finances while persuing higher education.

Fee Help scholarship Eligibility Criteria:

  • Student must get admitted to Parul University through ACPC quota.
  • Students maintain an average of 70% marks after receiving the scholarship

Fee Help Scholarship Rewards and Benefits:

  • University dicounts 30% of the tuition fees for the whoel duration of study.

Fee Help Scholarship Application Process:

  1. Apply to Parul University through ACPC portal.
  2. If you receieve admission the scholarship will be granted automatically.
