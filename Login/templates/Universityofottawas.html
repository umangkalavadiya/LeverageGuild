U of Ottawa Differential tuition.

Key Information:

The diffrential tuition scholarship offers quite impressive packages to any international student willing to take their whole course in French.

Differential tuition scholarship Eligibility Criteria:

  • Student must be a international student and not a resident of Canada
  • Have a GPA of 70 percent.
  • Has choosen French as their primary langauge of instruction.

Differential tuition Scholarship Rewards and Benefits:

  • University dicounts 80% of the tuiton fees of student selected for differerntial tuiton.

Diffrential tuition Scholarship Application Process:

  1. Apply to University of Ottawa and choose French as primary language of instruction.
  2. Satisfy the French language requirement.
